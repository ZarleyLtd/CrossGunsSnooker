Rules

CrossGuns Snooker League Rules and Format

1. Format and Entry Fees 

  • The entry fee for the CrossGuns League is €50 per player. 
  • All entry fees collected will be distributed in full as prize money. 
  • All players must register in advance to secure entry into the league. 
  • Entry fees are payable in full prior to the commencement of the competition. 
  • Table Light Fees:    
    • €12 per hour (Monday to Friday)    
    • €8 per hour (Saturday and Sunday) 
  • The table time will be billed from the moment the light is switched on, which includes practice time prior to the match, the match itself, and any post-match frames played.

2. Match Structure 

  • Each player will be scheduled to play one match per week. 
  • All matches up to and including the semi-finals will be played as best of three (first to two frames). 
  • The final will be contested as best of five (first to three frames).
 

3. Match Rules 

  • Matches shall commence promptly at the scheduled time. 
  • Standard snooker rules, as defined by the World Professional Billiards and Snooker Association (WPBSA), will apply.
 
Club Miss Rule (Club Rule – Must be followed in all matches)
A miss may be called up to a maximum of two (2) times. (Max of 3 fouls final foul can’t be called a miss)
After the third foul  play must proceed.
The incoming player then has the option to:
(a) Play from the position in which the cue ball has come to rest, or
(b) Ask the opponent to play again from that position. 
 
  • In the event of a dispute, the decision of the league organizer is final. 
  • Players are expected to conduct themselves in a respectful and sporting manner at all times. 
  • Failure to attend a scheduled match without prior notice may result in forfeiture

4. Handicaps and Match Results 

  • All handicaps and match results must be recorded on the official scorecard provided and signed by both players upon completion of the match. 
  • Handicaps will be reviewed after  every 4 matches  during the competition with no review during the knockout stages 
  • Any errors or omissions on the scorecard will render the result void all scorecards must be signed off and handed into the club and pic of scorecard posted . No rematches will be permitted in such cases. (If in doubt get your handicap confirmed before break off from Clive or Andy ) 
  • Current handicaps will be displayed on the club noticeboard and in the official WhatsApp group. 
  • It is each player’s responsibility to ensure they are aware of their correct handicap. In cases of uncertainty, players must contact the league organizers before commencing their match. Remember when signing off the scorecard your signing off on your own handicap and your opponent’s handicap too.

High Break Prize

The High Break Prize will be awarded based on each player's break score plus their handicap. Players with a handicap below scratch will have their break counted as made, with no subtraction applied.

To qualify for the High Break Prize, a player must make a break of 25 or higher before their handicap can be added to the total break score.
